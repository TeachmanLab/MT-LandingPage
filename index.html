Feeling Anxious?
Change your thinking.

What You'll do

1. Tell us about yourself

We’ll ask you to answer some questions about who you are and how you’re feeling.

2. Picture yourself in different situations

You’ll be asked to mentally put yourself in different situations.

3. Practice thinking in new ways

As you read about different scenarios, we’ll help you think about them in a healthier way.
